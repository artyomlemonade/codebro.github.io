Purpose of donation

As we are non-profit project which aims to help people from all of the world to attain heights in the field of IT, we need a funding to offer this opportunity to every human-being on Earth. Your donation will be considered as a contribution to the future generation full of great innovations and amazing discoveries.

Why do we need a donation ?

Although we have a tuition for course, our team is aware of the fact that not every human has the capability to pay indicated amount of money. Therefore, we are openning a program based on your funding where prospective students will be accepted.
